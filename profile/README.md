# Normanhurst Boys High School's Programming Club: 2026
Welcome to NBHS' Programming Club Github organisation. This is where we share and collaborate with code and work on projects, and where assignments and competition tasks are due.

## General Info
We meet every Tuesday in Room No. 39 at Normanhurst Boys High School's TAS department.
EOIs for NBHS students open March every year, and a CV and performance in a written examination is required for entry into the club.

Timeline and course specifications for Programming Club: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1MuWDjnYi8VT3pnbPDVXc84z_acWE0hG_hxQndlhwrMI/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.nrnw03t7conb

## Competitions
We take part in multiple competitions to accelerate students beyond the standard technology curiculum taught at high schools, and to develop their skillset. This includes the:
* AIO
* Stem Videogames Challenge
* UNSW ProgComp (Cupcake)
